Seamless BibTeX reference management for Overleaf and other modern LaTeX editors.

The title field is saved for the title of a work to be cited. LaTeX-specific writing, such as capitalisation, should be taken into account.

example#


@article{...,    title={What is bioinformatics? An introduction and overview},}

full example#




@article{luscombe2001bioinformatics,  title={What is bioinformatics? An introduction and overview},  author={Luscombe, Nicholas M and Greenbaum, Dov and Gerstein, Mark},  journal={Yearbook of medical informatics},  volume={10},  number={01},  pages={83--100},  year={2001},  publisher={Georg Thieme Verlag KG}}
